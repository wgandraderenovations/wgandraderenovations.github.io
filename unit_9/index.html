WG Andrade Renovations

Our Company

With over 15 years of experience in the market, WGAndrade is the leader in Comercial and Residential renovations. Working in the sector for a number of years, the Company stands out in the areas of painting, installation of air conditioning, electrical installation and cleaning.

Our Company is located in the heart of Sao Paulo, one of the biggest cities in the South America continent. The easy access to every part of the city provides confidence to the client that the service will be completed on time.

Services we provide:

We provide a number of services including:

  • Ar Conditioning
  • Commercial and Residential Cleaning
  • Commercial and Residential Painting
  • Electrical

Our history begins in 2000, when the Owner/Director Ezequiel Andrade retired. Ezequiel worked in a Global organisation for 38 years as a Civil Engineer. Just right after his last day working as an employee he decided to be an employer, hence WGAndrade Renovations started. Today, Ezequiel employs 10 employees and a number of indirect contractors.
